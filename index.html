We Can Help

Legal Aid Alberta assists eligible Albertans facing legal issues by helping them navigate their journey through the justice system and find lasting resolution to their legal challenges.

Check Your Eligibility Contact Us

What You Need

When applying for Legal Aid services, either in person or by calling 1.866.845.3425, please bring the following information to the interview:

  • Personal identification (eg: driver's license)
  • Documentation that verifies yours and your spouse/partner's financial situation (eg: last pay stub, bank statements, income support statements)

Depending on your legal issue, Legal Aid Alberta may require additional documents:

  • Family Law: Court dates in place; court applications/claims you have made; legal documents you have been served with
  • Criminal: Criminal charge documents (eg: promise to appear, undertaking to a peace officer)
  • Immigration: Immigration documents (eg: basis of claim)
  • Other legal matters: any documentation relating to your legal matter

Calgary only accepts in-person applications at the courthouse

Services Offered

  • We provide coverage for:
  • Family Law
  • Income Support
  • Adult Guardianship Issues
  • Serious Criminal Charges
  • Youth Criminal Justice
  • Child Welfare
  • Emergency Protection Orders
  • Domestic Violence
  • Service to the Siksika Nation
  • Certain Immigration Issues
  • Duty Council
  • We do not offer services in:
  • Landlord/Tenant Issues
  • Uncontested Divorces
  • Divorces Not Involving Children or Support
  • Personal Injury
  • Consumer Rights
  • Lawsuits Including Foreclosure & Civil Claims
  • Employment Issues
  • Wills & Estates
  • Bankruptcy
  • Third Party Applications
  • Immigration Sponsorship
  • Crime Victims
